Departments in Vardhaman college of Engineering

Vardhaman College of Engineering features diverse engineering and management departments, offering B.Tech, M.Tech, and MBA programs. Core branches include Computer Science and Engineering (CSE), Artificial Intelligence & Machine Learning (AI&ML), Data Science, Information Technology (IT), Electronics & Communication (ECE), Electrical & Electronics (EEE), Mechanical, and Civil engineering.These academic divisions provide comprehensive, industry-aligned education. The CS and IT branches offer specialized study tracks tailored for emerging technologies, supported by modern centers of excellence Vardhaman College of Engineering. The core engineering branches—such as ECE, EEE, Mechanical, and Civil engineering—focus on robust technical fundamentals, practical laboratory exposure, and research.
